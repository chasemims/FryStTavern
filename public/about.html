Fry Street Tavern

Originally called 'The Tavern of the Green' is a well-known establishment located in Denton, Texas, near the University of North Texas (UNT) campus. Established in 1995, it has historically been a popular hangout spot for college students, especially those attending UNT and Texas Woman's University.

Here are some key details about Fry Street Tavern:

  1. Location: Fry Street Tavern is situated on Avenue A, formerly known as Fry Street, which is a historic and vibrant area in Denton, known for its nightlife and proximity to the university.
  2. College Atmosphere: It has a lively and college-oriented atmosphere, making it a favorite spot for students to unwind, socialize, and enjoy drinks with friends.
  3. Variety of Drinks: The tavern typically offers a range of alcoholic beverages, including beer, cocktails, and more, making it a suitable place for those looking to relax and have a good time.
  4. Community Events: The tavern has been known to participate in community events and often has special deals or promotions, especially during college-oriented occasions.
